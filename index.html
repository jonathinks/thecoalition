The Coalition for Education System Upgrade

We are a group of young individuals who are not satisfied with our own experience as young learners. The problem with education is quite clear, we have an educational system designed for an age we we have never lived in, and it is doing more damage than good for many learners around the world. We are exploring solutions and building tools for learners of the Connected Age. We are not providing a single solution, rather working our own passion projects, sharing perspectives and looking for synergies of how these many projects can form the basis for a new education system as the old is falling apart.

We believe the key in solving big challenges is to apply a systems thinking lens, to see in interactions between actors and what flow and feedback loops drives the system. Our diversity therefore becomes very useful as we come together one a week across 5 time zones to share our perspectives and ask for feedback. Our backgrounds and expertise cross the boundaries between business, design, engineering, mathematics, political science, teaching and software development. If you would like to share your project ideas or give feedback to others, please write an e-mail to hello@jonathannylander.se.
